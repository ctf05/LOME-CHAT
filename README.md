## Our Principles

### Privacy First

We believe your conversations with AI are deeply personal. They contain your ideas, your questions, your work. We will never sell your data. We will never train on your conversations. We will never monetize your privacy.

**Local-Only Mode:** For maximum privacy, create conversations that never touch our servers. Your messages go directly to the AI provider and are stored only in your browser. We can't see them. We can't store them. They're yours alone.

### Radical Transparency

We charge a **5% fee** on AI model usage. That's it. No hidden fees. No premium tiers that unlock basic features. No "free" tier subsidized by selling your data.

You pay for what you use. We take a small cut to keep the lights on. The math is simple and public.

### No Data Monetization

We will never:
- Sell your conversations to third parties
- Use your data for advertising
- Train AI models on your chats
- Share your information with data brokers

This isn't a marketing promise‚Äîit's our business model. We make money from the 5% fee. We have no incentive to monetize your data because we've built a sustainable business without it.
